Hi, my name is Advika Shali and I am an aspiring game developer!

I am currently enrolled in Staffordshire University for a BSc in Games Developement. I am also self taught in creating digital illustrations and have been in love with the arts since 2012.

To me, video games are not just a form of entertainment to pass time, but an art form that has the power to transport a player to a foreign, make believe world where players can create a new version of themselves and hide away from the real world. I am constantly challenging myself as an artist, coder, designer and student and I am excited to dip my feet into this ever-evolving industry by sharpening my skills and getting exposure to the best industry practices at the esteemed Staffordshire University.

My long term goal is to make designing & developing games into a career in order to share my passion for building exciting new experiences with consumers and gamers everywhere.
